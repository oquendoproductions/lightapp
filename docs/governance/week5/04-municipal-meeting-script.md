# Week 5 — Municipal Meeting Script (Pilot Intro)

## Opening (2 minutes)
- "CityReport is an accountability engine for infrastructure your city controls."
- "Pilot scope is intentionally narrow: potholes and street signs."
- "We are measuring response workflow, not launching a public campaign."

## Problem Framing (3 minutes)
- Current challenge: intake exists, but measurable lifecycle transparency is inconsistent.
- Pilot goal: track reports to open/fixed/reopened outcomes with deterministic exports.

## What We Are Testing (5 minutes)
- Domain A: potholes (proximity aggregation).
- Domain B: street signs (asset aggregation).
- Shared contract: lifecycle events + snapshot + export schema `v1`.

## What You Will See (7 minutes)
- Public and internal view separation.
- Open Reports operational queue.
- Export summary/detail CSV with metadata headers.
- Reopen/chronic behavior on recurring incidents.

## What We Need From You (5 minutes)
- One sponsor, one backup.
- Weekly 20-minute check-in.
- Feedback on workflow clarity and reporting confidence.

## Scope Boundaries (2 minutes)
- No additional domains during pilot.
- No metric formula changes during pilot.
- UI clarity improvements are allowed; structural logic stays stable.

## Success Criteria (3 minutes)
- Faster and clearer triage decisions.
- Reduced ambiguity in open vs fixed state reporting.
- Confidence in exported data for internal review.

## Close (1 minute)
- "If this pilot is successful, we can discuss domain expansion using the same governance contract."
