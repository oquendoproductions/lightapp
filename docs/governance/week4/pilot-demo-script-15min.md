# CityReport Pilot Demo Script (15 Minutes)

## 0:00-1:00 Opening
- Problem statement: issues are reported across calls, memory, and paper; tracking gets fragmented.
- Positioning: CityReport is an accountability engine for municipal workflows.

## 1:00-4:00 Citizen Intake (Pothole)
- Submit a pothole report from map click.
- Show required flow speed and simple intake.
- Confirm report appears in Reports for admin.

## 4:00-7:00 Staff Intake (Water/Drain)
- Submit a water/drain incident as staff.
- Choose subtype: `sewer_backup` or `storm_drain_clog`.
- Call out source tracking (`municipal_staff`) and subtype capture.

## 7:00-10:00 Aggregation + Lifecycle
- Show proximity aggregation for potholes/water-drain.
- Open incident details, then mark fixed with note.
- Show state transition and closed visibility.

## 10:00-12:00 Accountability Outputs
- Open metrics cards and explain each metric.
- Show open vs closed filtering in Reports.
- Show source-aware reporting posture (citizen vs staff) in internal outputs.

## 12:00-14:00 Export + Audit
- Export summary CSV and detail CSV.
- Verify schema metadata/version header.
- Explain deterministic UTC windowing and audit logging.

## 14:00-15:00 Close
- Restate pilot scope: potholes + water/drain issues.
- Streetlights are utility-information only (not municipal SLA metric domain).
- Confirm 30-60 day pilot measurement plan.
