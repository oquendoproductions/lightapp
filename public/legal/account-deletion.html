Account Deletion

Effective date: May 1, 2026

Support: cityreport.io@gmail.com

CityReport.io offers self-service account deletion for resident accounts. You can start the deletion flow from inside the mobile app or from the web app while signed in.

Delete Your Account

Use the form below to sign in and permanently delete your resident account. Organization staff accounts may be blocked from self-service deletion until access is reassigned.

Return to homepage

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your login account and saved profile information are removed.
  • Your resident notification preferences and saved push tokens are removed.
  • Historical report records may be retained for municipal accountability, audit, and service-history purposes.
  • When those historical records are retained, identifying resident fields are scrubbed or anonymized.

Organization Staff Accounts

If your account is tied to municipal or organizational staff access, self-service deletion may be blocked until role ownership is reassigned. In that case, contact cityreport.io@gmail.com.

Need Help?

If you cannot access your account but still need deletion assistance, email cityreport.io@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account and include Account Deletion Request in the subject line.
