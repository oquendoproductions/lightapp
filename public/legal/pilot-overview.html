CityReport.io Municipal Pilot Overview

This page summarizes the publicly shareable pilot governance structure used for municipal evaluation and procurement readiness discussions.

Pilot Scope

  • Domains: potholes, water_drain_issues
  • Streetlights are utility-information only (not municipal accountability SLA scope)
  • Aggregation model: proximity-based for both pilot domains

Pilot Duration

  • Recommended pilot window: 30 to 60 days

Intake Sources

  • Citizens
  • Municipal staff
  • System-generated records
  • Tracked source values: citizen, municipal_staff, system

Municipal Operations Workflows

  • View incidents by status (open, closed, all)
  • Search and filter by date range and query
  • Mark incidents fixed with operational notes
  • Export summary/detail CSV for review

Data Ownership and Access

  • Municipal pilot data remains tenant-scoped in Supabase
  • Public/internal visibility follows Model C governance controls
  • Internal views preserve detailed operational context

Success Criteria

  • Reduced average time-to-fix
  • Lower reopen rate
  • Identification of chronic locations
  • Improved clustering and triage efficiency

Expected Pilot Outcome

  • Demonstrate measurable operational accountability
  • Produce defensible metrics for procurement review
